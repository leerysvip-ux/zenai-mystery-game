About The Red Eye Files

The Red Eye Files is an interactive emotional mystery universe. Each story begins with a strange event, a locked room, a missing message, or a door that should not exist. But the deeper you go, the less it is about fear — and the more it becomes about memory, loss, and goodbye.

Series 01: Final Goodbye is the first 50-episode chapter. Every choice unlocks a different ending. Every ending leaves behind a fragment. And every fragment brings you closer to the truth.
